About us

Our Mission

Our mission is to help programmers learn, share and improve themselves in the world of algorithms and competitive programming.

Our Story

Starting off from college classrooms, we went to the world wide web. SIESGST arena was founded in 2016 by Omkar Prabhu. Our first classroom contest was held in October 31,2015. Our platform was launched on November 2018.
